# Competitor Research - Moving Estimator Apps

## Research Date: Feb 2026

---

## 1. MoveAdvisor (moveadvisor.com)
- **Type:** Moving company reviews & cost estimator platform
- **Key Features:** Mover reviews, company ratings, cost estimator tool, BBB/FMCSA verification
- **Strengths:** 12+ years of data, consumer reports, state-by-state mover directory
- **Weaknesses:** No AI/camera scanning, no inventory management, purely review-based
- **Pricing:** Free (ad-supported)
- **Gap:** No visual inventory, no room scanning, no packing calculator

## 2. Sortly (sortly.com)
- **Type:** General inventory management (not moving-specific)
- **Key Features:** Barcode/QR scanning, photo uploads, custom fields, folder organization, CSV/PDF export, cloud sync, low-stock alerts
- **Strengths:** Excellent inventory UX, multi-device sync, offline support, team collaboration
- **Weaknesses:** Not designed for moving estimation, no cost calculator, no truck recommendations
- **Pricing:** Free (100 items) â†’ $29/mo Advanced â†’ $59/mo Ultra â†’ Enterprise
- **Gap:** No moving-specific features, no AI detection, no cost estimation

## 3. Unpakt (unpakt.com)
- **Type:** Moving quote comparison platform
- **Key Features:** Compare moving quotes side-by-side, book online, fixed pricing
- **Strengths:** Instant quote comparison, transparent pricing, easy booking
- **Weaknesses:** Manual item entry only, limited to their mover network, no AI scanning
- **Pricing:** Free quotes, commission-based from movers
- **Gap:** No camera scanning, no AI, tedious manual inventory

## 4. Muval (muval.com.au)
- **Type:** Full-service moving platform (Australia)
- **Key Features:** Interstate/local removalists, electricity/gas connections, removals insurance, self-pack containers, car transport, pet transport, packing/unpacking services
- **Strengths:** Comprehensive service ecosystem (utilities, insurance, pet/car transport), 60,000+ moves, 4.8â˜… rating
- **Weaknesses:** Australia-only, no AI scanning, no visual inventory
- **Pricing:** Quote-based
- **Gap:** No camera/AI features, region-locked

## 5. Moved (moved.com)
- **Type:** Property management moving coordination platform
- **Key Features:** Team dashboards, resident move coordination, utility connections, integrated with property management
- **Strengths:** B2B integration, automated workflows
- **Weaknesses:** Not consumer-facing, no inventory/estimation features
- **Pricing:** Enterprise/B2B

## 6. MovingWaldo
- **Type:** Moving concierge platform
- **Key Features:** Address change automation, utility transfers, service setup, moving checklist
- **Strengths:** Automates tedious admin tasks, utility connections
- **Weaknesses:** No inventory management, no cost estimation, no AI
- **Gap:** Only handles admin side, not physical inventory

## 7. HireAHelper
- **Type:** Moving labor marketplace
- **Key Features:** Hire movers by hour, loading/unloading help, truck rentals
- **Strengths:** Flexible hourly pricing, vetted movers
- **Weaknesses:** No AI scanning, basic inventory input
- **Gap:** No visual estimation, no comprehensive planning tools

## 8. General Market Gaps Identified
- **No competitor combines AI camera scanning + full planning suite**
- Most apps are either: inventory-only OR quote-only OR checklist-only
- None offer: packing calculator, QR labels, weight distribution, seasonal pricing
- Insurance estimation is always a separate service
- No app provides disassembly notes or fragile item flagging
- Progress tracking on moving day is completely unserved
